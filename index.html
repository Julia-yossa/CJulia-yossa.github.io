Julia Yossa

Queen Lia

Agripreneur - Nappy - Techie

I am presently in incubation for the enable youth Cameroon program at the IITA Cameroon station and I am looking forward to exciting entrepreneurial challenges.

I have a bachelor’s degree in Physics and computer science from the University of Buea. My business interest are Agriculture, renewable energy and new technologies. Born in a market, my father was a vendor and I was though how to sell and count money at 6 years old during holidays. I was a shy kid then but took courage and toured the market with my sweets basket to complete my sales before 3 pm every day. When I was much older, I helped my dad sell fresh products at his market space, I acquired negotiation and client communication skills.

I had my first formal job at 16 years old, I worked for Terre et developpement, an NGO specialised in environmental development as rural plannificator. We spend 3 months in Banka community, west region of Cameroon collecting information using specific tools in villages. It was an enriching experience, I came to discover many aspects of village life and the challenges faced by the rural population. Team work and empathy was what I took from the most.

The next job I had was a teaching job at King Solomon Anglo-Saxon section Ndi Samba, a private secondary school. I though physics and computer science to first cycle students. Enriching experience which lasted a trimester, it taught me a great deal of patience, planning and tolerance. After that, I had a part time surveyor job at Cible etudes et conseils. We had to survey customers of goods and services some companies offered. I worked on a total of 3 projects.

I am a volunteer at The CARE foundation, an NGO which offers free medical screening and surgical operations to internaly displaced and poor populations. I do a little bit of training; my firens and colleagues say i am good at research.

I love technology and I am passionate about renewable energy, creating businesses, teaching and cooking. Apart from my computer abilities, event organisation and food processing are some more of my skills. Did I even mention I produce and, sell flower and fruit syrups, my specialities are Hibiscus and pineapple syrup, you can have a taste as from 1,000 FCFA here. Coming soon are jam and oils (clin d’oeil).
