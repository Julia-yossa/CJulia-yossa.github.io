WHAT WE DO

JY Foods is a youthled enterprise focused on fruits and vegetable transformation. We deliver quality products without additives and artificial conservatives to adequately serve consumer needs. Based in Yaoundé, we are close to local consumers and available for global consumers. We find passion in providing better and safer food with a vision of sustainable development goals. We believe that agriculture, energy and new technologies are stepping stones for a prosperous economy.

Our main product is fruit and flower syrup for the local market. Made from whole fruits and flowers and conditioned in a germ free environment, we offer a taste of nature to our consumers. All you need is add water at convenience, can be used in desserts and cocktails for a priceless moment. Available flavours are hibiscus, pineapple, lime, ginger, mint, coffee and seasonal fruits. Online order is possible via Email and free delivery is offered in Yaoundé as from 20 bottles ordered.
